Welcome to the EEXCESS-Wiki-Recommender!

This extension provides useful information in form of images and articles while you write your Wikipedia article.

Activate the Extension

To get access to this information you only have to activate the extension by pressing on the EEXCESS icon in the menubar while you write your Wikipedia article. Then the extension gets started. That the extension is running is indicated by a 'on'-flag on the EEXCESS icon and its sidebar displayed to the right of the article editor.

Search Query Submitting

Then you can submit a search query into the sidebar by the following methods:

  • Search-Bar

    Submit a search query into the search-bar at the top of the sidebar and press the submit-button.

  • Enter

    Hit enter during writing your article. Then suitable content for your current paragraph is searched automatically. The generated query is also inserted into the search-bar to enable an adaption of it.

  • Selection

    Select text with the mouse and press the shown search button to search suitable results for the selection. The generated query is also inserted into the search-bar to enable an adaption of it.

Result Item Usage

The found search results can then be viewed in detail and inserted into the article at the current cursor position.
