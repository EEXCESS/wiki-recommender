Welcome to the EEXCESS-Wiki-Recommender!

This extension offers an easy and useful way to enrich your articles with the necessary image or biographical sources - all searched for and inserted by the click of a button.

Activate the Extension

After installation, the extension is activated per default. You can de- or re-activate it by clicking on the menu bar item . Alternatively, you can trigger the extension using the shortcut "Alt+Shift+W" for all major operating systems. If the shortcut doesn't work for you, you can change it using the shortcut configuration offered in your extensions overview.

Search Query Submitting

You can submit a search query into the sidebar using the following methods:

  • Search-Bar

    Type a search query into the search-bar at the top of the sidebar manually and press the submit-button.

  • Enter

    Simply hit enter during writing/editing your article. Keywords are extracted out of the context of the current paragraph and are searched for automatically. The generated query is also inserted into the search-bar to enable you to adapt it further.

  • Selection

    Select text with the mouse and press the appearing search button to get results matching your selection. The generated query is also inserted into the search-bar to enable you to adapt it further.

Result Item Usage

The search results can then be viewed in detail and inserted into the article at the current cursor position.
